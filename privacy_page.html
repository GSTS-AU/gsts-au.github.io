Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

Information Collection and Use

Glenn Stampalia Technology Solutions (GSTS) collects personal information for the fulfillment of quotes, orders, and the provision of technology solutions and services. We may retain and use this information for any such purposes.

Information We Collect

You are required to provide personal information to us for authorized purposes including:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, address)

  • Payment and billing information

How We Use Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to other persons for the purposes of:

Information Protection

We will not disclose your personal information without your consent unless authorized by law. Your personal information will be held securely and you can contact us to request access or correction.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as form processors, email providers, or analytics tools) that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

Accuracy of Information

We rely on you to submit correct information and details where requested. You accept that you may incur additional expenses if you submit incorrect information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website.

Contact for Privacy Matters

For any privacy-related inquiries, to access or correct your personal information, or to make a complaint, please contact us using the contact form on our website.
